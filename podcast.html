About the Podcast

Looking Beyond
the Obvious

Between the Lines is a psychology podcast that looks beyond the obvious.

Each episode explores one question about human behavior. We look at what research says, connect it to real experiences, share personal perspectives, and end with a small challenge you can try in your own life.

We don't just live life on the surface. There's always more going on between the lines. — Mo
