Episode 01

Why We Procrastinate

Why do we procrastinate even when we know it will only make us more stressed?

In the first episode of Between the Lines, Mo looks beyond the idea that procrastination is simply laziness or poor time management. The episode explores emotion regulation, fear of failure, perfectionism, distraction, and why beginning is often the hardest part.
