24 hours in one room. One singular room. 4 walls. 1 door. 1 window. 24 hours. Watching the birds. Watching the ceiling. Watching people pass by living their own lives. But here I am stuck in one room. 24 hours. One room. Playing games. Watching tv. Drawing. Painting. Hearing the chatter outside. Hearing laughter. But here I am stuck in one room. 24 hours. One room. Four walls. One door. One window. 24 hours.

That's when the clock struck midnight. The door opened. I was free. No more of that one room. 4 walls. 1 door. 1 window. 24 hours. But can I tell you a little secret? I’m real. So take me with you. And let me read this book with you. I know how to read. I promise.
