Ordinary

1 min read · By Mo

He was just a normal person most people wouldn't recognize him. He was too normal. Too sane. Too nerdy. Why would anyone notice someone like him? All he ever wanted was to be noticed because everyone always overlooked him because he was too quiet. Too shy.

But the crazy thing is…

He was a serial killer everyone had been looking for. So if I were you. I’d close this book.
