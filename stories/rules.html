Rules

1 min read · By Mo

Everyone follows the same rule. You must be inside by 10:30pm. No one knows what happens if you stay out later. No one has ever dared to test because we’re all too scared of what might happen if we do.

The second it gets a little too close to 10:30 everyone is scrambling. Running. Driving crazy to get home and get inside. And one thing I’ve come to realize is that no one remembers why we follow this rule.
