Silence

1 min read · By Mo

It could be loud or quiet. Soft or hard. Awkward or comforting. Yet it was all the same thing. Silence. Why could one word be felt in so many different ways? It’s not like the meaning of the word changed. It’s all about the situation and how you decide to interpret it.
