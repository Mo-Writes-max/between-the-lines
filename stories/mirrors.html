Mirrors

1 min read · By Mo

I turned around and didn't recognize the person in the mirror. The person I so carefully put together to make sure everyone would like me. The person I worked so hard to create. Why after all this hard work did I not recognize myself? I should be able to see myself for who I am through this mirror but it’s like it's one way.
