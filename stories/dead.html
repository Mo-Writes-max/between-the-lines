Dead?

1 min read · By Mo

I told myself I was fine and my body disagreed. But I couldn't figure out why. I felt ok. Nothing was wrong with me. I only had a couple of small scratches. So why was my body disagreeing with me? Was it because I was dead?

It’s always the quiet ones. Watch out.
