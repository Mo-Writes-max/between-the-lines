Casket

1 min read · By Mo

It’s only when I'm lying in my casket that I see my husband that has cheated on me for years, crying and weeping, saying that he’s sorry for everything he’s done and he wished he was there for me more. To see my parents and my siblings crying and sobbing like they were ever there for me but of course now that I'm gone everyone is here crying, giving me flowers, saying their condolences. I wish I could stand up and say that no one was here for me when I was alive so why be here now but I can’t do that. I can only lay here and listen to everyone talk about me and say how I was a great person and how I should still be alive.
