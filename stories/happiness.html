Happiness

1 min read · By Mo

This is a letter I wrote but never got the chance to send.

I realized I didn't miss you, I missed who I was with you. I was happy. Cheerful. I was myself. But now that you’re gone, I see how much you really took from me. And I want it back. I want to go back to my old self. The cheerful, happy person that I was.

If only I didn’t kill you.

Love, Darla
