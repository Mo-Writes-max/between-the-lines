Writers

1 min read · By Mo

Writing. I enjoy writing. The characters, the plots, I enjoy writing. The stories I made, I enjoy writing. No one can tell me that I'm wrong because I know I'm not the only one who can hear it. I enjoy writing. Everyone can hear it. Why is it just me? I’m only writing a story and making fictional characters, so why are they talking to me? I enjoy writing, this shouldn’t be happening. This needs to stop. They’re on paper, they aren’t allowed to talk. I ENJOY WRITING. So make it stop. Even after I close the book. I throw away the paper and they still talk to me. I ENJOY WRITING, SO MAKE IT STOP
