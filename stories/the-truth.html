The Truth

1 min read · By Mo

I've always known I was meant for more. To have a greater life.

That’s why I have such a successful business and I’m rich. I built something for myself. I wanted to be someone that was known by many and loved by even more. The trips I get to take around the world to try and make a positive impact on people’s lives.

Is what I would say... if every word I just said wasn’t a lie.
