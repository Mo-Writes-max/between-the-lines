Hallway

1 min read · By Mo

Being a therapist can be a rewarding but hard job because you get to help people get better mentally, however not everyone is the same and people have different ways of coping. But recently I noticed that there was a recurring thing that kept showing up in all of my sessions. Every patient that came into my office described the same hallway they saw in their dream. The lights, the same accent wall, and even the smell. Which I found a little odd……Then I remembered it was the same hallway I died in.
