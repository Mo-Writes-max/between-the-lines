Newly Weds

1 min read · By Mo

It was finally the day I had been waiting years for. The day I get married to the woman of my dreams, the love of my life. She made me feel whole. Feel seen. Like I was special and I couldn't have chosen a better woman to spend the rest of my life with. Everyone stood up and the music started playing for her to walk out. I was frozen, when I imagined her coming down the aisle, I didn't think she'd be in a casket.
