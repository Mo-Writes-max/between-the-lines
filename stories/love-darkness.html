Love & Darkness

1 min read · By Mo

I've always enjoyed the light, the joy, the happiness. The comfort in being able to see the light and know what’s on the other side. To spend time with the person I loved most. The one I cherished and held dear to my heart. The one I would do anything for. Yet I still got betrayed and this time left for dead. But it wasn’t until my very last dying breath did I hear her say “We all did it” And I have to say it was a hell of a way to die. But for once the darkness was comforting.
