Problems

1 min read · By Mo

I realized that I was the problem. Why everyone would leave me. Why no one ever wanted to be my friend. I was the problem all along. But for so long I couldn't see that, always putting the blame on other people like it was their fault. Why did it take me so long to see that? I just wanted to have friends like everyone else did. Go out and have fun like all the people do on social media. Taking trips, getting coffee, and going to parties. Everyone around me seemed to belong somewhere. But I didn’t.
