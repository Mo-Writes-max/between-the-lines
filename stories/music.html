Music

1 min read · By Mo

It’s a way to escape from reality. To feel something else. To have something to relate to. It’s almost like someone is talking to you when all you want to do is break down and cry. Like a pair of arms is wrapped around you and holding you to make sure you’re okay. They say all the right words needed for that moment and you find it comforting to have someone there even if it’s only just a song.
