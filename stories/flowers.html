Flowers

1 min read · By Mo

The flowers died on Monday. The day I lost everything that mattered most to me. The day everything came crashing down like a storm in the middle of summer. But why me? It could have been anyone else in this world yet it happened to me. Did I do something to deserve this because I don't feel like I did. But then again it’s my life so there's no one to blame but myself I guess.
